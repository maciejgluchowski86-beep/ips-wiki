# Patch critical density for FA-1f

For the hard [FA-1f model](fa-1f-model.md) with \(N(i)\ne\vn\), the [patch critical density](patch-critical-density.md) equals the equilibrium density \(p=1-q\) of ones:

$$
p^\star=p.
$$

## Proof

The only nonzero coefficients with nonempty interaction target are

$$
c_i^0(N(i))=-p,
\qquad
c_i^1(N(i))=-q.
$$

The coefficient formula for the patch critical density therefore gives

$$
p_i^\star
=
\frac{c_i^0(N(i))}
{c_i^0(N(i))+c_i^1(N(i))}
=
\frac{-p}{-p-q}
=
p.
$$
